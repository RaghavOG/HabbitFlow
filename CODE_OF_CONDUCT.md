# Code of Conduct

We want HabitFlow to be a welcoming and respectful place for everyone.

## Our expectations

All contributors and community members are expected to:
- Be respectful and constructive in discussions.
- Assume good intent.
- Keep feedback focused on the work, not the person.

## What we won’t tolerate

We don’t tolerate harassment, hate speech, or disruptive behavior of any kind.

## Reporting issues

If you believe someone is violating this code of conduct, please open an issue describing the concern.
